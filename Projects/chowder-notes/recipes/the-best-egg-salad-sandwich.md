## Instructions

1. Place chilled, peeled hard-steamed eggs in a medium bowl.
2. Add mayonnaise, lemon juice, lemon zest, celery, scallions, and parsley.
3. Using your hands, squeeze the eggs through your fingers, breaking down the yolks into a creamy mixture while keeping chunks of egg white intact.
4. Continue mixing until the salad reaches your desired consistency. Alternatively, smash and mix with a firm whisk.
5. Season generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
6. Serve as sandwiches on bread with sliced radishes and lettuce, or as desired.

## Notes

- Crushing eggs by hand gives the best texture: creamy yolk-mayo mixture with large tender pieces of white. A firm whisk works as a hands-free alternative but may over-break the whites if used too aggressively.
- Scallions are preferred over red onion, white onion, or shallots for a milder, sweeter crunch.
- Lemon juice and zest are key to brightening the richness of the mayonnaise — don't skip them.
- Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
