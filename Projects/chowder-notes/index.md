# chowder

## What this is
Chowder is a voice-first household dinner steward for iOS/macOS. Its purpose is to absorb the cognitive burden of feeding a household — deciding what to eat, what to buy, and how to cook it. The ultimate goal: make dinner feel inevitable. After initial setup, a household should be able to say "Chowder, take care of dinner" and trust that it will happen — quietly, reliably, with as much or as little involvement as they want.
