# Gravity
#### Normal gravity
Use a RigidBody2D with a Sprite2D and CollisionShape2D

It’s possible to reverse gravity in a specific area to get a “push up” effect

#### Planetary gravity
Use a planet (example in [this video](https://youtu.be/TyXx3B1_lhA?si=JqV7WTkOutvdv-qP)) to simulate pulling things in
